Our Approach

ERA is a strategic advisory firm that provides independent, seasoned judgment to global CEOs at the moment of decision.

Europe is not just a market — it is a regulatory and geopolitical force that shapes global operating conditions regardless of whether a company has EU presence. Decisions taken in Brussels drive capital allocation, supply chains, competition policy, sanctions, industrial strategy, ESG standards and corporate reputation worldwide.

The core risk is rarely lack of information, but fragmented interpretation. ERA integrates political, regulatory and geopolitical signals into clear strategic judgment — with direct implications for financial exposure, risk management and capital allocation — at the exact moment it is needed.

This is grounded in direct experience of real decision-making environments, not open-source monitoring. ERA interprets how political and regulatory dynamics translate into market, funding and risk parameters before they generate public consequences — including second-order effects on financing conditions, hedging strategies and balance sheet resilience. ERA does not execute, represent or sell access. Its independence is structural.
