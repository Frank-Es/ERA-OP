Our Approach

We help global CEOs anticipate how Europe will shape their business — before it becomes a constraint.

Europe is not simply a market. It is a regulatory and geopolitical force increasingly defining global operating conditions. Decisions taken in Brussels or European capitals influence capital allocation, supply chains, competition policy, sanctions, industrial strategy, ESG standards and corporate reputation worldwide — including companies with no direct EU footprint.

The real risk is rarely lack of information, but fragmented interpretation. Political, regulatory and reputational exposure is often assessed in silos — internally and across advisors — without an integrated decisional view. ERA operates at that integration point, transforming dispersed signals into clear strategic judgment for the CEO.

We combine structured analytical frameworks with institutional-level insight grounded in direct experience of real decision-making environments — not open-source monitoring — to interpret real decision dynamics before they become public consequences. We do not execute, represent, or sell access. Our independence is structural.
